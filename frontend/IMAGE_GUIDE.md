# Image Guide - Ramsay App
## Airbnb-Style Visual Guidelines

### General Style Principles
- **Clean & Minimal**: Lots of white space, uncluttered compositions
- **Natural Lighting**: Soft, diffused light (not harsh shadows)
- **Warm Tones**: Slight warmth in color temperature
- **Human-Centric**: Show people using the product naturally
- **High Quality**: Sharp, professional photography
- **Composition**: Rule of thirds, leading lines, balanced framing

---

## Page-by-Page Image Guidelines

### **Onboarding 1 - Welcome/Ramsay Logo**
**Purpose**: First impression, brand introduction

**Image Concept**:
- Clean, minimal shot of the Ramsay logo "R" in a physical space
- Could be: Logo on a desk with natural light, or a simple geometric representation
- **Alternative**: Abstract financial/growth visual - coins stacking, money growing, or minimalist financial chart
- **Style**: Very minimal, lots of white space, single focal point
- **Colors**: Black logo on white, or subtle gradient
- **Mood**: Inviting, professional, trustworthy

**Specs**:
- Aspect Ratio: 1:1 or 4:3
- Focus: Center or rule of thirds
- Lighting: Soft, even lighting

---

### **Onboarding 2 - How It Works Flow**
**Purpose**: Visual explanation of the process

**Image Concept**:
- Three-step visual flow showing the journey
- **Option A**: Three connected icons/illustrations in a row (Deposit → Vault → Earn)
- **Option B**: Split screen showing before/after or progression
- **Option C**: Animated-style still showing money flowing into a vault
- **Style**: Clean infographic style, minimal text, visual storytelling
- **Colors**: Black and white with subtle accent (if any)

**Specs**:
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 or 4:3
- Layout: Horizontal flow or vertical stack
- Elements: Icons, arrows, simple illustrations

---

### **Onboarding 3 - Security/Wallet Control**
**Purpose**: Build trust, show security

**Image Concept**:
- **Option A**: Close-up of a physical wallet with key, showing ownership
- **Option B**: Abstract representation of security - lock, key, or safe
- **Option C**: Minimalist illustration of a wallet card with key icon
- **Style**: Clean, trustworthy, secure feeling
- **Colors**: Black and white, maybe subtle metallic tones for wallet/key
- **Mood**: Secure, professional, empowering

**Specs**:
- Aspect Ratio: 1:1 or 4:3
- Focus: Wallet/key as hero element
- Lighting: Soft, professional product photography style

## Technical Specifications

### Image Formats
- **Primary**: SVG (for icons/illustrations), PNG (for photos)
- **Fallback**: WebP for photos
- **Size**: Optimized for web (under 200KB per image)

### Dimensions
- **Onboarding**: 800x800px (1:1) or 1200x800px (3:2)
- **Welcome**: 1920x1080px (16:9) or full screen
- **Fund**: 1200x800px (3:2)
- **Processing**: 800x800px (1:1)
- **Success**: 800x800px (1:1)
- **Dashboard**: Full screen or pattern tile

### Photography Tips
1. **Lighting**: Use natural light or soft studio lighting
2. **Background**: Clean, minimal, lots of white space
3. **Composition**: Rule of thirds, leading lines
4. **Depth**: Shallow depth of field for product shots
5. **Color**: Keep it minimal - black, white, subtle grays
6. **Post-Processing**: Clean, professional, not over-edited

### Illustration Tips
1. **Style**: Minimalist, line-based, geometric
2. **Colors**: Black and white only
3. **Thickness**: Medium stroke weight (2-3px)
4. **Shapes**: Simple, geometric forms
5. **Composition**: Balanced, centered or rule of thirds
